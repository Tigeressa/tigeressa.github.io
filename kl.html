Climate change is perhaps the clearest indication of how our ordinary, everyday actions negatively impacts the health of the planet. The Kinder Living blog aims to provide valuable insight into the dangers of the consumer lifestyle, to challenge bad habits, and to share suggestions on how to live each day with more care for the environment around us.


As indicated by the example homepage below, the blog adopts a classic, recognisable layout structure. It is also fully responsive so it can be read, referenced and enjoyed across multiple devices.
